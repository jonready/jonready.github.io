About

Jonathon Ready is an undergraduate student at Pennsylvania State University majoring in Computer Engineering.

He is the founder of Penn State Unmanned Aerial Systems Team - an interdisciplinary undergraduate team that competed in the 2017 AUVSI Student UAS competition. Supporting his teammates he raised over $20,000 in parts donations and funding from multiple sponsors including Boeing and FLIR. Within the organization he has lead the systems architecture development, which incorporates: software, electrical, and mechanical systems on the unmanned system. He is the pasionate about improving systems, and facilitating interdisciplinary inquiry.

Jonathon is also a big fan of puzzles and as such wrote a fun script to solve the January 9th NPR Sunday Puzzle .

In his spare time, he enjoys brewing traditional Chinese tea, and visiting tea farmers.
